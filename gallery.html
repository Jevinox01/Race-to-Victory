The Toyota GT86 is a compact sports coupe known for its agile handling and driver engagement. Launched in 2012, it features a 2.4-liter flat-four engine, producing around 228 horsepower, with options for a six-speed manual or automatic transmission. With a lightweight design, rear-wheel drive, and a low center of gravity, the GT86 offers an exhilarating driving experience, making it a favorite among car enthusiasts. Its sleek, sporty design adds to its appeal as an affordable sports car.
